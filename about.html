About Christu Jyothi Institute of Technology and Science

CJITS refers to Christu Jyothi Institute of Technology and Science, an engineering college located in Jangaon, Telangana, established in 1998 under the Warangal Diocesan Educational Society. It is affiliated with JNT University, Hyderabad, approved by AICTE, and accredited by NAAC. The college offers various engineering and computer science courses and has a focus on holistic student development, providing placement assistance and a range of campus facilities and activities.

CJITS is committed to excellence in technical education and producing highly skilled engineers who contribute to society.

Vision

Produce engineering graduates fully equipped to meet the ever growing needs of industry and society.
